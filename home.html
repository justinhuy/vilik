ABOUT US

VILIK (VIK) is a socialFi video live streaming platform built on the blockchain. Our product delivers fast, high definition streaming to our viewers, for a variety of purposes beyond simply just entertainment content. Viewers and streamers can also earn crypto tokens just by using our platform. Users and streamers can earn tokens, mint and trade NFTs, donate and more. The possibilities are endless, and the tokens you earn can be used to participate in the NFT market and NFT games.

VILIK (VIK) aims to utilize available open source software to build a decentralized and more efficient architecture, which powered by the Binance blockchain, can greatly reduce operating costs and allow for infinite scalability
